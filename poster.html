2022 MaRDA Meeting

The aim of this year's MaRDA meeting is a taking a deep dive into the MGI 2.0 strategic plan, and connecting researchers within the field. I presented a poster on the accelerated discovery of in-plane ordered MAX phases (i-MAX). This type of material is of particular interest because its magnetic properties and the superior electrochemical properties of its 2D derivatives, dubbed i-MXenes. In this work, I performed high-throughput DFT calculation of the stabilities of compounds within the OQMD framework with different M, M', A and X elemental combinations. The I successfully identified many compounds that are thermodynamically stable but have not yet been experimentally synthesized.
